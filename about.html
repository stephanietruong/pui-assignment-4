Our Story

Bun Bun Bake Shop was started 10 years ago as a result of Stephanie's passion for baking. She loved eating the classic cinnamon rolls her grandma used to make for her as a child. She decided to use her imagination to add different or even sometimes odd ingredients into the classic cinnamon roll to create a new flavor.

We started out with baking cinnamon rolls from our own kitchen and shipping it world-wide. Two years ago, we were able to have a physical location. We now have a small bakery that specializes in cinnamon rolls but we have also tried to expand our product lines.

Our shop is located in Pittsburgh, PA, but we still continue to ship fresh rolls to anywhere in the USA.
